Privacy policy

  • This page provides information relevant to the methods of managing the processing of personal data of users who log in Chiara Ferragni Collection Website (the following “User” or “Users”) that can be found at the address www.chiaraferragnicollection.com (hereinafter the “Website”). It is information given in compliance with art. 13 of the Law Decree of 30th June 2003, no. 196, “Code for protecting personal data” (hereinafter “Privacy Code”).

    1. Personal Data Collector

    The Data Collector is Serendipity Srl (hereinafter only “Serendipity” and/or the “Collector”), with offices at via Melone 2, 20121 Milano.

    2. Purposes of the processing and type of personal details

    The personal details that the User voluntarily provides when he/she logs in to the Website, such as name, surname, password, addresses, phone numbers, e-mail addresses, tax code, VAT number, credit card, bank details may be used by the Collector for the following purposes:

    i)               registration and orders management, which means managing the User’s logging into the Website and dealing with the User’s orders and/or providing services relevant to the orders such as, for example, the creating of a “Wish List”;

    ii)             newsletter subscription, which means managing User’s subscription to the newsletter service by Chiara Ferragni Collection, automatically receiving every new issue or newsletter and other commercial and promotional communications for free;

    iii)            profiling purposes, which means processing profiles based on the preferences and the purchases of the User in order to send information focused on the User’s interests and needs.

    The User‘s provision of data for the purpose of registering with the Website and order processing, as set out in the above point a), is necessary to perform the obligations under the contract with the User and/or to fulfil the specific requests of the same, and therefore the Holder does not require the User’s express consent for processing the data. Failure to provide the data required for the purposes set out in the above point a), however, will mean that the User will not be able to register with the Website, manage or process any orders or be provided with the related services, including, but not limited to, the creation of a “Wish List”.

    With reference to the purposes of subscribing to the newsletter and profiling, as set out in the above points b) and c), the provision of data is optional and processing of the same by the Holder can only be carried out with the User ‘s specific and express consent. The User’s refusal to the processing of the data for the purposes set out in the above points b) and c), means that the Holder will not be able to process the data supplied for the purposes of subscribing to the newsletter and profiling, but it is still possible for the User to register with the Website, manage or process any orders or be provided with the related services, including the creation of a “Wish List”.

    Please take note that pursuant to art. 130, paragraph 4, of the Privacy Code, the Collector may use the details relevant to the e-mail that the User has provided during the sale of a product or the supply of a service, for offering services similar to those that are subject of the sales and without getting the User’s authorization. At any moment, the User can refuse the processing of data easily and free of charge by following the methods given in the paragraph 5 “Rights of the concerned person.”

    3. Method to process the data

    The User’s data is processed mainly by means of electronic systems and sometimes in hardcopies.

    The User’s data will be kept with a method that allows the identification for the time strictly necessary to achieve the purposes for which the data is collected and processed and, in any case, in accordance with law provisions.

    The Collector and the responsible part have adopted suitable security measures in accordance with the law to prevent the loss of Data, unauthorized access or improper or illegal uses.

    The User’s data may be transferred to foreign countries (also extra-EU countries, such as USA), in compliance to the above-mentioned information.

    4. Categories of subjects to whom the personal data can be communicated or who can know it and the diffusion of personal data

    In order to achieve the purposes that the personal data is collected for, the Collector can appoint other responsible parties for processing the personal data such as IT service providers, i.e. direct marketing, internet service and cloud computing, subjects that are in charge of the warehouse logistics, as well as promotion, sale and delivery of the products and services of the Collector, customer care, companies and other subjects that provide customers with legal, fiscal, accountancy, financial, technical-organization, data processing and communication services. Subjects that provide bank services, i.e. financial, insurance and credit recovery services, controlled companies, subsidiaries, public authorities and supervising bodies.

    In order to get the complete list of subjects responsible for personal data processing, the User can contact the Collector in accordance with the methods given in the following section 5 “Rights of the concerned persons”.

    For the same purposes, Chiara Ferragni Collection and the parties responsible for processing can appoint other responsible parties for processing data such as employees in charge of the execution of orders, the customer care, the IT and marketing services (if the authorization is given), the IT systems and management systems of the Website and the technical and commercial personal in charge of providing the website services.

    No User’s personal data collected from this website will be diffused.

    5. Right of the concerned person

    Pursuant to art. 7 of the Privacy code, the Users can, at any time, request information about their personal data, if it exists, its contents, origin, correctness or ask for its integration and update or correction. In accordance with the same article, the User is entitled to request its deletion, transformation in an anonymous form or the block of his/her data which is processed in violation of the law and, in any case, the User can, for legitimate reasons, oppose himself/herself to its processing.
