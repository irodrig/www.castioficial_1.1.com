In a world full of creativity, technologic advances , of multicultural progress we keep on defining an after in the story of humanity but it’s also true that very time more, leaving on a side situations that truly are in need of attention and a change in the world at an educational and conscientious level.

Casti believes in the revolution of “Heal the world”.

After going around to the already existing concepts of sustainable fashion, fast or slow fashion, we got to the point where none of this came close to the move that Casti needed to express and that’s where Healty Fashion was born. A concept that encompasses our social commitment and our desire to help in an active way various causes to make the world a better place. We firmly believe in the brand we built, we know that fashion with its imposing world presence moves masses and that for a grain of sand all big constructions begin.

Healthy fashion represents this change of consciousness, a new concept that makes that our brand carry a message through the runway and that last in the lives of the ones we are Casti and we make from each staging tell a story of a new beginning.
